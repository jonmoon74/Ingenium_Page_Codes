Continuing your learning

Now that you have completed all of your initial learning modules, there will be some additional learning that is relevant to you based on the professional family you are part of.

This page has been put together to help you identify what further learning will support you in your role. You should use the details provided to guide you in preparing your learning plan and then discuss this with your line manager through the Aspire check-in process.

You may find that your role covers more than one of the tabs below, in which case please browse all the relevant links to identify the learning relevant for you.

  • Dealing with payments
  • Client Focus
  • Payroll
  • People Management

If your role includes handling food, or managing people who handle food, then please check through this playlist to select the learning you need to be compliant with Sodexo Food Safety policy.

Food Operations Learning

This playlist is of the learning modules associated with handling payments within a Sodexo Unit. The content covers both cash and non-cash payments, as well as using Sodexo systems to process takings etc.
The modules are intended for those handling the payments directly as well as line managers of people taking payments.

Client Focus learning

If, as part of your role, you will need to input data to the payroll for your unit, you will need to complete the Managing your unit payroll training.
To begin this training you will need to have the payroll access form from SodexoNet fully approved before you can complete the training pathway, so please speak to your local HR business partner as well as your line manager before enrolling.
Once you have the agreement to undertake payroll training, use the link to access the learning. You should see the correct learning for your region, but if you do see two versions please check you are enrolling on the correct one. The RoI version is only suitable for colleagues in the Republic of Ireland.
