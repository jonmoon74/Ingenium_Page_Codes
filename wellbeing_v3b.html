Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.
Mental health is important at every stage of life and affects each person differently. This is why it is important for all individuals in the workplace to be aware of potential contributing factors to mental health issues and strategies to maintain a positive pathway to mental health and encourage early reporting of symptoms.

Anyone can suffer from mental health problems, no one is immune and people with mental health problems often face stigma, which can prevent them from seeking help and hinder their recovery.

What is Micro Learning?

Micro-learning is more than simply bite-sized training modules. Micro-learning is focused and offers you just the right amount of information necessary to help you as a learner achieve a specific, actionable objective. Micro-learning is easily accessible on your mobile and tablets, on-demand, sociable and fun!
