In support of our commitment to ethics and business integrity, we launched Responsible Business Conduct training for all managers two years ago. This year, all Sodexo managers are required to demonstrate their understanding of Responsible Business Conduct through the completion of refresher training around key topics: anti-bribery and corruption, data protection and data security, and sexual harassment.

All Sodexo UK & Ireland managers must complete the RBC refresher and Corporate Criminal Offences training before 31 March 2021.
